From an administrator's perspective, HighlandPetvibes begins with a secure Login that requires authenticated admin credentials to protect sensitive business and customer information from unauthorized access. The admin Dashboard serves as a centralized page, nagpapakita ito ng vital metrics tulad ng total customers, appointments, shop orders, statistics, at recent activities for business oversight.

Through the admin Appointments module, pwedeng i-check ng mga administrator ang lahat ng pet appointments, i-filter ang mga ito ayon sa status (Confirmed, Pending, o Cancelled), i-toggle ang visibility ng past appointments, at i-delete ang bookings if needed. Ang admin Shop section ay nagbibigay access sa mga administrator to oversee all retail operations, manage inventory, and track orders for pet-related products and services.

From the admin Customers section, maaring ma-access ng mga administrator ang complete database where they can add new customers, profile editing, outdated record deletion, at ma-access ang detailed pet information, streamlining customer relationship management. Ang admin Reports section ay nagbibigay ng comprehensive business analytics sa pamamagitan ng specific charts at service breakdowns, pati na rin ng detailed appointment histories na nagpapakita ng booking patterns.

Lastly, the admin Settings page, may access ang mga administrator sa iba't ibang features: Notifications panel for message overlays, Security sub-tab para sa credential management, Business sub-tab para sa core business information, at Scheduling sub-tab para sa appointment availability optimization.
